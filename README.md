# Oil Empire! Calculator

A free, open-source planner & optimizer for the **Oil Empire!** idle game.
Plan the best drill layout, simulate purchases, and squeeze maximum income out of every zone.

**Live site:** https://thekayrawastaken.github.io/oil-calculator/

## Features

- **12-zone × 9-slot grid** with the real game's multipliers (3×, 5×, 2×, 1×)
- **Drag & drop** drills from your inventory into matching slots (2×2, 2×1, 1×1)
- **Auto-Place**: one click fills every zone with your optimal layout
- **Investment Suggestion**: enter a budget (e.g. `500B`, `2T`) and get a recommended buy plan
- **3-Way Calculator**: solve any of Gas / Money / Time given the other two
- **Machine Calculator**: how long until you can afford X copies of a drill
- **Machine Comparator**: purchase combos against each other by income delta
- **Save Setup / Load Setup**: download/upload your placements as a JSON file
- **Share**: download a copy of the page with your data baked in, send it to a friend
- **English & Turkish** interface
- **Saves locally** in your browser — no servers, no tracking

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Hover any slot | `X` | Disable / enable that slot/drill |
| Hover a 2×1 slot | `C` | Split into two 1×1, or merge back |

## How to Use

1. Set your in-game **Sell price** and **Bonus** at the top.
2. Use **+ / -** on each chip to record how many drills you own.
3. Drag chips into slots, or click **Auto-Place** to let the optimizer decide.
4. Click the **✓** in a zone corner to disable a whole zone; press **X** while hovering to disable a single slot.
5. Click the green **Quick Tutorial** button (bottom-left) for an in-app walkthrough.
